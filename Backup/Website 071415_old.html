Exercise 1

Why is Alyssa taking this class?

I have enrolled in this class to become a UX Designer. Four years ago I started to explore transferring my art skills to the tech industry.

I enrolled in evening classes at a community college near the San Francisco Bay Area, taking both Web Design and Game Design classes. It was during these classes I was first introduced to computer programming.

One day in class, my professor was walking past my desk and asked what I was doing. "I'm just tweaking the code," I replied. He looked at me with a strange expression and said, "You know, it is unusual for an artist to have no fear about diving into code. You should consider being a programmer. The industry needs programmers who a visual talents."

Thus, began my journey to becoming a computer programmer.
